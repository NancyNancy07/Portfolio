Foofest Copenhagen

Final study Project

In this assignment, I created an app for Foofest Festival based in Copenhagen. In this project, I mainly focused on javascript frameworks that's why I developed this app in React. The main purpose of this project was creating a full ticket purchase flow.
There are some special features were created for example- Selecting the ticket and having the counting of tickets in basket, selecting the camping area, Personal Information form and saving the infirmation in netlify, Credit Card form.
